# Armor Echo

Armor Echo is an action-driven, arcade tank combat game developed with the **Unity Engine** and programmed in **C#**. Players operate heavy armored vehicles, navigating tactical terrain maps to engage, outmaneuver, and destroy hostile forces.

## 🛠️ Engineering & Development Stack

*   **Physics Controllers**: Rigidbody-driven tank movement designed to emulate realistic track friction, turret rotation inertia, and chassis recoil upon weapon discharge.
*   **Game Loop & Systems Architecture**: Implements GameManager patterns to regulate match states, wave-spawning behaviors, and UI score overlays.
*   **Weapon & Damage Pipeline**: Built with modular weapon scripts, supporting variable shell velocity, explosive splash radiuses, and direct impact damage.

## 🚀 Key Systems

*   **Enemy AI Tactics**: Simple pathfinding logic that positions enemy tanks to flank the player, find cover, and calculate ballistic trajectories.
*   **Combat Feedback**: Custom particle systems for muzzle flashes, projectile trails, and destructible impact debris to enrich visual feedback.
*   **Level Design Integration**: Built with environmental obstacles that block projectiles, forcing players to use tactical positioning and movement to survive.
